About Us

why choose us?

HypeTicket is an initiative undertaken to generate electronic tickets which are QR Code based.
It is an Online Web application aimed at people who are looking to visit Monument and Historical Places.

Our objective here is to reduce the hassle of printing out tickets on site as well as allow the staff at Heritage places to do better crowd management. This system will not only save your time in queues but also reduce paper wastage as a bonus.
